About Me

Hi, I'm Ashley Gigon a mechanical engineer with a strong foundation in physics and a passion for innovative problem-solving. I earned my B.S. in Physics from Providence College, with a minor in Business and Innovation. I then completed both a B.S. and an M.S. in Mechanical Engineering at Columbia University, specializing in Robotics and Control Systems.

My experience spans government, research, and industry, including roles at the Naval Undersea Warfare Center and the von Karman Institute in Belgium. I've worked on everything from submarine tactics and naval simulations to wind tunnel testing and automated robotic systems. I'm skilled in tools like SolidWorks, MATLAB, and Python, and I enjoy applying these to both research and real-world applications.

I'm passionate about engineering design, robotics, and developing systems that push boundaries.

Outside of work, I love running, traveling, and always looking for new opportunities to grow, learn, and make a meaningful impact.
